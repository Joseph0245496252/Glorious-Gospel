About the College

The Main Aim of the Coolege

The main aim of this life changing Bible Institution is to equip servants of God and the Saints, by offering sound, practical, Biblical and Ministerial training from an inter-denomination position also to adequately equip servants of God such as Apostle, Prophets, Evangelist, Pastor, Teacher and Christian or Church workers with the emphasis on soul winning church planting church growth, and evangelism with a balanced doctrinal knowledge, above that, also train them on how to study the Bible, interpret the Bible and preach the Bible effectively without any compromising (II Timothy 2:15)

The Entry Requirement

An entry requirement for the certificate is J.H.S and above or having the ability to read and write. Diploma is a Certificate from a recognized Bible College, and Bachelor is a Diploma from a recognized college with an academic transcript attached to the admission form, incomplete will be rejected.

Tenets of Faith

Even though the collage is non-denominational, but the Collage strictly believe and have her own tenet of Faith. The College believes the Bible as a total inspired word of God. The external existence in one God in three persons (TRINITY). The College believes in the virgin birth of our Lord Jesus Christ, His crucifixion, buried, raised from the grave to Heaven The College believes the justification, regeneration being only received by faith in Blood of Jesus Christ and Sanctification as a subsequent to the new birth through faith in the Blood of Jesus Christ. The College believes in the Lord’s Super as an act of a remission of sin and new covenant. The College believes in Rapture, Tribulation, The Millennium Reign, Battle of Armageddon and the Eternal judgment into Hell and Heaven.

Programmed Offered

Our collage offers Certificate, Diploma in Biblical Studies and Bachelor of Arts, Master’s degree and Doctorate in theology, Christian Education, Ministry and Church Management.
